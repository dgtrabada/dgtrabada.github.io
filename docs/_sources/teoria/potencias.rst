*************************
Cuestionario de potencias
*************************

.. cuestionario::

   1. Calcula y memoriza las siguientes potencias de dos:
      filas: ancho=9em
         2^{0} = | [1]
         2^{1} = | [2]
         2^{2} = | [4]
         2^{3} = | [8]
         2^{4} = | [16]
         2^{5} = | [32]
         2^{6} = | [64]
         2^{7} = | [128]
         2^{8} = | [256]
         2^{9} = | [512]
         2^{10} = | [1024]

   2. Calcula las siguientes potencias de dos (utiliza un punto para escribir los decimales):
      filas: ancho=9em
         2^{-1} = | [0.5]
         2^{-2} = | [0.25]
         2^{-3} = | [0.125]
         2^{-4} = | [0.0625]

   3. Calcula el resultado, puedes escribirlo en potencia de 2, por ejemplo:
      | 2^{2}*2^{3} = 2**5, o poner el resultado directamente: 2^{2}*2^{3} = 32
      filas: ancho=9em
         2^{9}*2^{9} = | [2**18|262144]
         2*2^{9} = | [2**10|1024]
         2^{0}*2 = | [2**1|2]
         2^{9}/2^{8} = | [2**1|2]
         2^{21}/2^{9} = | [2**12|4096]
         2^{9}*2^{8}/2^{5} = | [2**12|4096]
         2^{0}*1 = | [1|2**0]
         2^{3}*2 = | [2**4|16]
         2^{3}/2^{2} = | [2**1|2]
         128*2 = | [2**8|256]
         1024/2 = | [2**9|512]
         32*32 = | [2**10|1024]
         64*64 = | [2**12|4096]

   4. Calcula las siguientes potencias de diez (utiliza un punto para escribir los decimales):
      filas: ancho=9em
         10^{-4} = | [0.0001]
         10^{-3} = | [0.001]
         10^{-2} = | [0.01]
         10^{-1} = | [0.1]
         10^{0} = | [1]
         10^{1} = | [10]
         10^{2} = | [100]
         10^{3} = | [1000]
         10^{4} = | [10000]
         10^{5} = | [100000]
         10^{6} = | [1000000]

   5. Calcula el resultado y escríbelo en potencia de 10.
      | Ayuda: para escribir 10^{11} pon 10**11
      filas: ancho=9em
         10^{9}*10^{9} = | [10**18]
         10*10^{9} = | [10**10]
         10^{0}*10 = | [10**1]
         10^{9}/10^{8} = | [10**1]
         10^{21}/10^{9} = | [10**12]
         10^{9}*10^{8}/10^{5} = | [10**12]
         10^{0}*1 = | [1|10**0]
         10^{3}*10 = | [10**4]
         10^{3}/10^{2} = | [10**1]
         10^{7}*10 = | [10**8]
         1000/10 = | [10**2]
         100*100 = | [10**4]
         10*100000 = | [10**6]
